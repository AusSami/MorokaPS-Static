Subjects Offered

At Moroka Primary School, we offer a diverse curriculum designed to cater to the holistic development of our learners. Our subjects include:

  • Mathematics
  • Natural Sciences and Technology
  • Creative Arts
  • Social Sciences
  • Economic and Management Sciences
  • Life Orientation
  • Life Skills
  • Languages — English, Sesotho, IsiXhosa

Each subject is taught by qualified educators who are passionate about fostering a love for learning and helping students achieve their full potential.
