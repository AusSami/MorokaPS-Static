About Moroka Primary School

Moroka Primary School is committed to providing quality education that empowers learners to reach their full potential. Our school emphasizes academic excellence, discipline, and growth in a safe and supportive enviroment.

Our Mission

English

    At Moroka Primary School, we commit ourselves to:
  1. Meet challenges through excellence in teaching and learning.
  2. Encourage learners to realise their full potential and become responsible contributors in society.
  3. Develop a sense of moral integrity in all situations and relationship.
  4. Foster a sound working relationship with the community through the involvement of learners, parents and staff.

Sesotho

    Sekolong sa Moroka Primary School, re ikemiseditse ho:
  1. Fumana diphephetso ka thuto e ntle le ho ithuta.
  2. Khothaletsa baithuti ho fihlela bokhoni ba bona bo felletseng le hore ba kenye letsoho sechabeng ka boikarabelo.
  3. Hlahisa maikutlo a botshepehi maemong ohle le dikamanong.
  4. Matlafatsa kamano e ntle ea mosebetsi le sechaba ka ho kenyelletsa baithuti, batsoali le basebetsi.

IsiXhosa

    ESikolweni saseMoroka Primary, sizibophezela:
  1. Ukuphumelela kwimfundo ngokufundisa kunye nokufunda ngokugqibeleleyo.
  2. Ukukhuthaza abafundi ukuba bafezekise amandla abo apheleleyo baze babe ngabaxhasi abanoxanduva kuluntu.
  3. Ukuphuhlisa umoya wokunyaniseka kuzo zonke iimeko nakwizinto ezinxulumene nazo.
  4. Ukukhuthaza ubudlelwane obuhle nomphakathi ngokubandakanya abafundi, abazali kunye nabasebenzi.

Our Vision

English:Moroka Primary School, aims at being an institution that provides holistic education and quality service through excellence, dedication, tolerance and integrity

Sesotho:Sekolo sa Moroka Primary School, se ikemiseditse ho ba setsi se fanang ka thuto e felletseng le tshebeletso ea boleng ka botle, boitelo, mamello le botshepehi

IsiXhosa:ISikolo saseMoroka Primary, sijolise ekubeni yindawo enikezela ngemfundo epheleleyo kunye nenkonzo esemgangathweni ngokugqibelela, ukuzinikela, ukubekeka kunye nokunyaniseka
